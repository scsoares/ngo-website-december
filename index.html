Up-Coming event

Reimage the Future of Education

Join our inaugural summit to explore innovations in education that empower students and create a more just, equitable and student-driven learning experience.

About the event

Connect All Humanity is proud to present the Future of Education Summit on October 5-6, 2023. This two-day event will unite a diverse community of leaders reimagining what an empowering, forward-thinking education looks like. Through panels, workshops, and sessions, we'll dive into personalized learning, cultivating creativity, leveraging tech for equity, community- centered classrooms, rethinking assessments, and designing optimal learning environments.

Key Themes

Throughout the two-day summit, we will hold focused discussions organized around six key themes that explore different facets of transforming education for the 21st century and beyond. From leveraging emerging technologies to fostering creativity in the classroom and beyond, these sessions will provide actionable insights from experts across disciplines.

Personalized Learning
Creativity & Innovation
Educational Equity & Access
Community-Centered Classrooms
Rethinking Assessments
Optimal Learning Environments
